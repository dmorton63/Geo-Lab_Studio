# Paint Mode Quick Start

## 🎨 5-Minute Guide to Manual Terrain Sculpting

### 1. Generate Initial Terrain
- Choose noise type: **FBM** for natural terrain
- Set scale to **4.0**, octaves to **6**
- Click **Randomize** for a new seed
- Let it generate

### 2. Enable Paint Mode
- Expand **"Paint Mode"** section
- Check ✅ **"Enable Paint Mode"**
- Notice: Auto-update turns off, 3D view disables

### 3. Choose Your Brush
Select from dropdown:
- 🔺 **Raise** - Build hills and mountains
- 🔻 **Lower** - Carve valleys and holes
- 🌊 **Smooth** - Soften rough areas
- ⬜ **Flatten** - Create plateaus

### 4. Adjust Settings
- **Brush Radius**: How big? (Start with **30**)
- **Brush Strength**: How fast? (Start with **50**)
- See white circle cursor? That's your brush!

### 5. Start Painting!
- **Left-click and drag** on the terrain
- Watch it change in real-time
- Paint in smooth strokes for best results
- Release and paint again to build up effects

### 6. Try Different Brushes
- **Raise** some hills in the middle
- **Lower** a valley between them
- **Smooth** the rough edges
- **Flatten** (set height to 0.6) for a plateau

### 7. View Your Work
- Click **"Exit Paint Mode"**
- Enable **"3D View"**
- Rotate camera to admire your creation!

### 8. Save Your Masterpiece
- Expand **"Export"** section
- Click **"Export as PNG"**
- Find it in your executable directory

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

**Big to Small**: Start with large radius for rough shapes, decrease for details

**Low Strength**: Better control with multiple light strokes than one heavy one

**Smooth is Magic**: Use after raising/lowering to blend everything naturally

**Check Different Views**: Switch between Height/Slope/Biome while painting

**Save Often**: Export periodically - no undo yet!

## 🎯 Quick Challenges

### Challenge 1: Volcano 🌋
1. Raise brush, large radius, build cone
2. Lower brush, small radius, create crater at top
3. Smooth the slopes

### Challenge 2: River Valley 🏞️
1. Lower brush, medium radius
2. Draw winding path across terrain
3. Smooth the banks
4. Check water mask to see water flow!

### Challenge 3: Terraced Hills 🏔️
1. Flatten brush, set height to 0.3
2. Paint first level terrace
3. Change to 0.5, paint next level up
4. Smooth between levels
